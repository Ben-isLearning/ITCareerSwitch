Convering Types in Python... 

Method: Turn this (...) into an integer
int() chops off whatever is after the Decimal point. - Doesn't round. 

int()

int(10.00)  >>> 10

int(28.17)  >>> 28


Method: Turn this (...) into a float

float()

float(22)  >>> 22.0

float(101) >>> 101.0


Method: Turn this (...) into a string 

str() 

str(101) >>> "101"

str(12.13) >>> "12.13"
